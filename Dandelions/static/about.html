A Gentle Beginning

It all started in a tiny rented house in 2018. A few volunteers, six children, and big dreams. We shared meals, crafted bedtime stories, and created bonds that still bloom today.

A Growing Family

By 2020, we welcomed elders and differently-abled friends into our family. The laughter of children met the wisdom of age and suddenly, we weren't just a shelter, we were home.

Our Mission

To build a safe place where love has no limits. A haven where stories are shared across generations, and every smile plants a seed of hope.

Looking Ahead

We dream of a world dotted with places like Dandelions where no one is left behind, and kindness grows wild and free like flowers in spring.

Thank you for believing in tender revolutions of love with us.

— The Dandelions Team
